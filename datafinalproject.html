Data Visualization

what are the factors that influenced the charge of patients, why this patient has high charge while other does not? The dataset I will be working with contains medical costs for a number of different patients characterized by certain attributes and some information about each patient.
I will going to try to predict the cost that a given patient will incur based on their region whether or not they're a smoker, how many children they have, their BMI, their sex, and their age. From this data, we will also see what factors influenced the charge of patients. Examples would be, Finding correlation between BMI and Charges, finding correlation between Smokers and Charges, Predict charges for new clients, etc.
